Motivation - Disclaimer

I have created these pages for two main reasons:
  • I use too many computers and Internet is one of my main information sources for my work, I always had to copy my bookmarks from one computer to another one. So I decided to put everything on the Web in order to easily get access to this data. If some other people have the same interests than me, they may find these pages useful, but the link lists are by no way a real directory:
    • I presume that several links are broken. If I find such a problem, I correct it, but I never check proactively that all the links are working.
    • The lists are not exhaustive, I just keep the links I use regularly or some I found useful at one time.
    If you find a broken link and you know a site that may interest me, just send me an email, I will look at it. Thanks.
  • I believe that Internet is one of the greatest inventions of the century. I really mean it: the Web is the biggest record of human knowledge existing today and it should be the task of everyone to help increase this huge amount of information. This is why I put some data I could not found somewhere else.
Since I am playing sometimes with HTML or JavaScript, some links are not accessible if you are not surfing with the right browser: I mean Netscape 4.0 or superior ;-) I am sorry for this inconvenience, but Microsoft and Netscape have diverged in their DHMTL implementations and I am not interesting in implementing twice the same stuff.

I must confess that some pages are completely useless (my resume, my ideas...). But without them, this would not really be a personal homepage...
