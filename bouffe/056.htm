langues de chat

makes 20 to 24

  • 50g butter
  • 50g caster sugar
  • 2 egg whites
  • 50g plain flour, sifted
  • vanilla essence
    • Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Whisk the egg whites lightly and gradually beat into the creamed mixture. Carefully fold in the flour and essence.
    • Place in a piping bag fitted with a 1 cm plain nozzle and pipe 7cm lengths on a greased floured baking sheet. Bake in a preheated moderately hot oven, 200°C, Gas mark 6, for 10 minutes; the biscuits should be pale golden but darker around the edges. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.


    source: "Home Baking" by Carole Handslip - page 54
