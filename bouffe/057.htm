nutty plum crumble

serves 4 to 6

  • 675g plums, halved and stoned
  • 50g sugar
topping:
  • 150g plain flour
  • 75g butter or margarine
  • 25g demerara sugar
  • 5ml ground cinnamon
  • 75g hazelnuts, toasted and chopped
  • Set the oven at 180°C/gas 4. Place the plums in an ovenproof dish and sprinkle with the sugar.
  • Make the topping. Sift the flour into a mixing bowl and rub in the butter or margarine until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. Stir in the sugar, cinnamon and hazelnut.
  • Sprinkle the topping evenly over the plums, pressing it down very lightly. Bake the crumble for about 45 minutes, until the topping is golden brown and the plums are cooked. Serve with custard, cream or vanilla ice cream.


source: "Complete Book of Puddings & Desserts"  - page 211
