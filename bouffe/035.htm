chocolate chip biscuits

makes 25 to 30

  • 125g butter or margarine
  • 50g caster sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 150g self-raising flour, sifted
  • 175g plain chocolate, chopped
    • Beat the butter or margarine and sugar together until light and fluffy. Add the egg and beat thoroughly. Fold in the flour and chocolate.
    • Place teaspoonfuls of the mixture a little apart on a greased baking sheet and bake in a preheated moderate oven, 180°C, for 15 to 20 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.


    source: "Home Baking" by Carole Handslip - page 28
