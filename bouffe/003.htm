cheese and onion oat flan



oat pastry:
  • 125 g wholemeal flour
  • 125 g medium oatmeal
  • pinch of salt
  • 125 g margarine
  • 2-3 teaspoons water
filling:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 ml milk
  • 250g Cheddar cheese, grated
  • salt and pepper

    •  
  • Place the flour, oatmeal and slat in a mixing bowl and rub in the margarine until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Add the water and mix to a firm dough. Turn onto a floured surface and knead lightly until smooth. Roll out and use to line a 20 cm flan dish. Chill for 15 minutes.
  • Meanwhile, make the filling. Heat the oil in a pan, add the onions and fry gently until transparent. Mix the eggs and milk together, then stir in the cheese, onions, and salt and pepper to taste.
  • Pour into the flan case and bake in a preheated moderately hot oven, 190°C, for 35 to 40 minutes. Serve hot or cold, with salad.
