drop scones

makes 12 to 16

  • 250g plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/2 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 250 ml milk
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Shift the dry ingredients together into a mixing bowl and make a well in the centre. Add the egg and half the milk and mix to a smooth batter. Gradually beat in the remaining milk with the oil, mixing to a thick batter.
  • Heat a heavy frying pan or griddle and grease lightly. Drop tablespoons of the batter onto the griddle and cook until the top is blistered. Turn with a palette knife and cook until the underside is golden brown.


source: "Home Baking" by Carole Handslip - page 40
