date loaf

makes one 1 kg loaf

  • 250 g dates, chopped
  • 175 ml cold tea
  • 250 g wholemeal flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground mixed spice
  • 175 g soft brown sugar
  • 1 egg
topping:
  • 1 tablespoon demerara sugar
    • Put the dates in a bowl, pour over the tea and leave to soak for 2 hours. Add the remaining ingredients and mix thoroughly.
    • Turn into a lined and greased loaf tin. Sprinkle with the demerara sugar and bake in a preheated moderate oven, 180°C, for 1 to 1¼ hours.
    • Leave in the tin for 5 minutes, then turn onto a wire rack to cool. Serve sliced, spread with butter.


    source: "Home Baking" by Carole Handslip - page 74
