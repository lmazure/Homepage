table:
  • to indicate that a cell is the fusion of several one, use the rowspan and colspan attributes of <TD>
    • <TABLE border=1>
    <TR><TD rowspan=2 colspan=2>big cell</TD><TD>small cell</TD></TR>
    <TR><TD>small cell</TD></TR>
    <TR><TD>small cell</TD><TD>small cell</TD><TD>small cell</TD></TR>
    </TABLE>
